Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Fan-made project notice: Free Fire Scholar is an independent, non-commercial fan project created purely for entertainment and community engagement purposes. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Garena, Free Fire, or Sea Limited in any way. No official scholarship, prize, or reward is issued by any game company through this site.

1. What This Site Does

This website offers a fun, interactive "verification" experience for Free Fire players — a quiz, an optional screenshot upload, and a shareable certificate. It exists purely for entertainment and nostalgia among the Free Fire community.

2. No Server, No Data Collection

This site does not have a backend server. There is no database, no account system, and no information is transmitted to us or stored anywhere outside your own device. Specifically:

  • Your UID and username are only used locally in your browser during your session and to prevent duplicate entries on the same device.
  • Any screenshot you upload is processed entirely inside your own browser and is never uploaded, sent, or saved to any server.
  • We do not collect emails, phone numbers, passwords, or any other personal or account information.

3. Local Storage

This site uses your browser's local storage only to remember, on your own device, which UID/username has already completed the experience once. This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared anytime through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Resources

This site loads some publicly available third-party libraries (such as font and icon libraries, and browser-based text/image recognition tools) directly from public CDNs to power certain features. These libraries run entirely within your browser and do not transmit your data to us or to any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children, because no information is collected or stored on any server in the first place.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to better reflect how the site works. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy or the site, feel free to reach out through the contact details listed on our homepage.
